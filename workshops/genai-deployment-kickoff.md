# GenAI Deployment Kickoff (60 minutes)

## Goal

Help teams move from prototype enthusiasm to production readiness with measurable outcomes.

## Structure

1. Problem framing and target KPI selection
2. Architecture sketch and risk model
3. Evaluation loop design (offline + online)
4. Rollout plan and ownership map
5. Runbook and rollback requirements

## Deliverables

- Initial architecture diagram
- Risk register
- 30-day rollout plan
- Evaluation and observability checklist
